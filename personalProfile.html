What do these tests mean for you?
The results of all three tests is an eye opener to me. I would like to take the test ten to fifteen times with each test asking different questions. I would also like to take the test at different times of the day as I believe the results may vary.
I think these test are good for individuals that are unaware of their strengths, weaknesses.

How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?
Myer-Briggs: The results of this test indicate I may put myself forward to lead the team towards a destination.
Learning Styles: This test indicates I prefer to meet with my team in person for meetings rather than audio communication. Adding visual aids to express my opinions will easier for me to explain a concept or situation.
Big 5 Personality Test: This test would indicate I will work hard to get the job done. I will be open to others opinions but I might fall out of touch with the teams feeling as my focus will be on the project

How should you take this into account when forming a team?
One team should have one lead, not multiple. A great team has a mix of varied individuals. It would be advantageous to form a team with individuals that have similar learning styles as you’ll be able to express your ideas in a way others can relate, in doing this other members of your team can quickly and easily assess the value of your statement and respond appropriately.
